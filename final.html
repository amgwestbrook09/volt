Volt's Backstory

Volt is a powerful hero living in a futuristic neon city filled with glowing skyscrapers and endless digital lights. Known for staying calm under pressure, Volt uses powerful electric energy to protect the city from danger.

Appearance

  • Normal human body
  • Cartoon-style glowing eyes
  • Lightning bolt mark on cheek

Personality

  • Calm and confident
  • Brave with powerful energy

City Overview

  • Futuristic neon city
  • Tall glowing buildings
  • Digital signs everywhere
  • Always lit up at night
